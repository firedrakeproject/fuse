compute_num_reps(base_val=0)¶

Compute the numerical represention of each member as compared to the identity. Where the numerical rep is:

M.index(id[0]) = a; M.remove(id[0]) M.index(id[1]) = b; M.remove(id[1]) M.index(id[2]) = c; M.remove(id[2])

o = (a * 2!) + (b * 1!) + (c * 0!)
